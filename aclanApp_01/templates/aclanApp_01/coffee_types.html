Coffee Types

Arabica

Known for its sweet, smooth taste and complex aroma. It's the most popular coffee type worldwide.

Robusta

Has a stronger, harsher taste with a grain-like overtone and peanutty aftertaste. Contains more caffeine than Arabica.

Liberica

Known for its distinctive flavor profile, often described as fruity and woody. It's relatively rare compared to Arabica and Robusta.
