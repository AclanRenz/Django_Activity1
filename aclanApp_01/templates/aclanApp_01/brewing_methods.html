Brewing Methods

Pour Over

A manual brewing method that involves pouring hot water over ground coffee in a filter. Produces a clean, flavorful cup.

French Press

An immersion brewing method that steeps coarse ground coffee in hot water before pressing to separate the grounds.

Espresso

A brewing method that forces pressurized hot water through finely-ground coffee to produce a concentrated shot.
